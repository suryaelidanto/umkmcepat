# Indonesian Business Website Context

Context for building websites for Indonesian small businesses (UMKM).

## Audience

- **Business owners:** Warung, laundry, kopi, bengkel, rental, katering, etc.
- **Their customers:** Local community, WhatsApp-based, mobile-first users
- **Tech level:** Most access via phone, simple navigation preferred

## Copy Rules

- Natural Indonesian, not formal/baku
- Use "kamu" not "Anda" for friendly tone
- Business name prominently displayed in hero
- Include: operational hours, location, contact method
- WhatsApp CTA: "Pesan via WhatsApp" / "Chat Kami" / "Hubungi Kami"
- Keep sentences short and direct

## Common Patterns

- **Hero section:** Business name + tagline + primary CTA (WhatsApp)
- **Services/offerings:** What the business does, clear and specific
- **Contact section:** WhatsApp button, phone, address
- **Operational hours:** When the business is open
- **Location:** Map or address (if applicable)
- **Testimonials:** Social proof from customers (if available)

## WhatsApp Integration

Most UMKM businesses use WhatsApp as primary contact:

- CTA button: "Pesan via WhatsApp"
- Link format: `https://wa.me/{phone}?text={encoded_message}`
- Phone format: `628xxxxxxxxxx` (country code + number)
- Pre-filled message: greeting + business context

## Content Guidelines

- Use real business data from the implementation spec
- No invented claims, prices, or guarantees
- Specific > generic (e.g., "Sewa PS Rp 5.000/jam" not "Harga terjangkau")
- Include operational details (hours, location, contact)
- Indonesian customer-facing copy, English for code/docs
